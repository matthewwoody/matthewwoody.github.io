Welcome!

I'm a junior majoring in Applied Math at Sonoma State University with an interest in Atmospheric Science which I'm set on pursuing at the graduate level. I hope this page serves to give you some insight into who I am!

Outside of school and work, I like to play hacky sack as well as work out/weightlift in general and am always on the lookout for new Middle Eastern food spots (thank you to Khan's Halal Cart outside of Jamaica Station for inspiring this in me- best chicken over rice of all time).
Passively, I'm a big time enjoyer of 90's and 2000's animated shows! If you're in the mood to pick up a new show, I absolutely recommend Batman: The Animated Series, followed by Batman Beyond; both of which are available for free on Internet Archive. These shows are incredible, the characters (even villains) are well-developed and we get to see their personalities and motives for why they are the way they are (beyond just being good or evil for the sake of it), the art styles are just timeless. In general these shows are overall mature and grounded in dealing without adult themes despite primarily being a kids show.
On the other hand, a definite kids show Codename: Kids Next Door is another recommendation of mine, although not quite as gripping as Batman, there is an interesting world built around their escapes as secret agents opposing "adult tyranny" and their interactions with one another in such a world. Recess walked so Codename: Kids Next Door could run.
